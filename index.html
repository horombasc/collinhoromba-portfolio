01 — About

About Me

I'm Collin Horomba, a graphic design student based in Vereeniging, South Africa. My work focuses on meaningful visual communication — using design principles to tell stories, honour heritage, and create clarity.

I believe good design is never accidental. Every spacing decision, font choice, and colour contrast is an intentional act of communication.
